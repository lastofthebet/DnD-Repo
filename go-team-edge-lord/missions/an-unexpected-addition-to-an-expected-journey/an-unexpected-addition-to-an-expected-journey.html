Bitter Frost
Bitter Frost

#Town #Ashvale_marches
Description: In the corner of the marching mountains lie the city of Bitter frost. Originally built to supply the great palisade with war material. Now it mostly supports Ashvale and surrounding towns.

Current faction: The Bitter Frost Family

Biome: Tundra, Subterrain

Features of the town

Sky district

The upper district in the mountain home less to foundries and tenement complexes. More to offices and noble housing for the elites of this Fort.

Infinite Blade works

Exceptional and artisanal that is IB blades currently the only forge still in the Sky district they arm the royal guards and Most of the Nobles all use axes forged by them.

Forest District

The middle district home to the great gates of Bitter frost. this is the middle class and tourist district. You need work, you need gear and your need a place to rest you'll find it here.

The Gate

The grand gate of Bitter frost is the only way out

The Port district

By Hammer and Heat

A stock mass production forge. cheap and effective is their motto. 75% of the axes and armour is produced by them.

Deep District

Skunk works

A black smith with a penchant for strange and effective gear will work for less if the work is interesting or challenging
